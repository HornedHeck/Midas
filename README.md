# 💰 Midas

> Track less. Understand more.

Midas is a personal finance tracker focused on clarity — import your bank data, see where your money goes, and stay in control. No cloud, no subscriptions, no noise.

---

## 💡 Key Ideas

- 🔒 **Privacy first** — all data stays on your device, always
- ⚡ **Minimal effort** — import transactions from your bank instead of entering them manually
- 📊 **Analysis, not just tracking** — understand your spending patterns, not just log them
- 🌍 **Free and open source** — transparent, community-driven, yours to inspect and extend
